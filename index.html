About us

Welcome To Jyoti Electronics

We do the things Much better!

Jyoti Electronics is a recognized techno-trading house based in India (Ahmedabad),
serving the Indian market since 1981 while diversified towards the distribution of
RF/Microwave design software & related hardware since 2000.

We provide a complete solution to the design software requirements by bringing
industry-standard CAD & EM tools which includes circuit simulation tools, system
simulation tools, EM simulation tools, & many more. These tools provide you the
best option to design & analyze any electrical or RF component covering the
frequency range from statics up to the optical regime.
We have a legacy to provide all sort of spaced qualified components including
cables, cable assemblies, connectors, a wide range of antennas, and all test &
measurement instruments.
